# Xianxia Sword Immortal and Celestial Fairy

Target runtime: about 5 minutes  
Recommended workflow: generate in short batches first, then expand to the full sequence

## Setup

- World: ancient Chinese xianxia realm with cloud mountains, suspended palaces, sealed ruins, sword arrays, moonlit pools, and broken celestial architecture
- Male lead: a young sword immortal in white and pale blue robes, calm, restrained, precise, carrying a dark sword
- Female lead: a celestial fairy in silver-blue gauze robes with a jeweled crown, elegant and unreadable, surrounded by moon-white spiritual light
- Visual direction: photoreal live-action Chinese fantasy film, not anime, not illustration, not game splash art

## Story

The forbidden Taixu secret realm is about to open. A young sword immortal is ordered to investigate the disturbance before the buried power inside can be seized. On the way, he meets a mysterious celestial fairy under the moon. Their first encounter becomes a duel, but the clash awakens an older catastrophe hidden beneath the water.

Once the secret realm opens, both are drawn into a shattered heavenly palace where the true enemy emerges: a fallen immortal remnant who wants to restart an ancient slaughter array. Forced into a temporary alliance, the sword immortal and celestial fairy move from suspicion to trust, combining sword light and heavenly radiance to destroy the remnant. When the palace collapses, he saves her from the void, and the story ends with both standing together at dawn, their bond unresolved but unmistakable.

# Xianxia Sword Immortal and Celestial Fairy

Target runtime: about 5 minutes  
Format: 30 chained live-action xianxia clips, each ending with a readable state for the next clip to inherit

## Direction

- Visual style: photoreal Chinese xianxia feature film, restrained high-end VFX, no anime or game-cinematic look
- Continuity rule: every shot should begin from the previous shot's motion, eyeline, light, debris, mist, or energy trail
- Character arc: suspicion at the moonlit pool, forced cooperation in the palace, deliberate trust before the final strike, quiet unresolved bond at dawn
- Palette: white, silver, pale blue, moonlight, black-gold corruption for the antagonist

## Continuous Beat Sheet

1. Dawn glides over cloud mountains and hanging bridges, ending on a cliff path where blue spiritual energy leads forward.
2. The sword immortal follows that energy onto the cliff, stops in the wind, and looks toward a hidden mountain temple.
3. An elder arrives with a golden decree; the decree burns a route across the clouds.
4. The route becomes his flight path as he rides a flying sword through lightning toward a moonlit valley.
5. He lands at a terrace beside a black pool where silver petals and a moon-white pulse draw attention across the water.
6. The celestial fairy is revealed on the far bank, turning toward the ripple rather than posing for a reset.
7. She tests him with a thin ribbon of moonlight, cautious and unreadable.
8. He half-draws his sword; their powers meet at the center of the pool without either side fully attacking.
9. Their first exchange deflects sword light into the trees and leaves them balanced on opposite wet stones.
10. They circle the pool with flying swords and ribbons, tightening the geometry of the duel.
11. Their powers collide above the water, and the falling spray reveals glowing seals underneath.
12. Both lower their weapons as the pool drains and the terrace splits around them.
13. The hidden realm opens below the pool, pulling stone and water upward into a vertical palace shaft.
14. They exchange a distrustful glance, leap into the collapsing opening, and land on separate suspended stairways.
15. Parallel paths through the upside-down palace reveal one distant altar before a broken wall divides them.
16. The sword immortal fights awakened stone warriors and ends behind a defensive fan of blades.
17. The fairy crosses an illusion terrace, tears open a passage with her ribbon, and moves toward the same altar.
18. Both paths reconnect at the altar; guardian puppets force them back to back.
19. Their first coordinated fight pushes the guardians toward the altar core.
20. The guardians shatter, the Taixu core rises, and a black-gold handprint appears inside it.
21. The fallen immortal remnant forms piece by piece from orbiting armor shards and cuts the altar light in half.
22. His thunder sword intent drives the sword immortal backward, leaving a blue gouge across the floor.
23. The fairy steps into that gouge and shields him with a moon-white celestial barrier.
24. The fallen immortal tries to divide them with dark script chains, but the closest chain cracks between their hands.
25. The sword immortal lowers his guard and turns his back to her, choosing trust.
26. She accepts, braiding silver ribbons around his sword light and opening a shared path forward.
27. Ten thousand swords rise into a blue dragon while her heavenly array anchors it.
28. The combined strike destroys the fallen immortal and the array core, sending debris toward the void.
29. The fairy falls first; he dives after her, catches her, and both spiral upward through clouded starlight.
30. Dawn returns on a snow peak; they stand side by side in silence as the camera withdraws, leaving the bond unfinished.

## Reference Notes

- Use the supplied dual-character reference for identity only at the beginning, not as a repeated scene reset.
- Keep faces, hairstyle, robe palette, and age consistent once established.
- Avoid fox ears, tails, animal traits, static portrait framing, and unrelated new locations between chained shots.
